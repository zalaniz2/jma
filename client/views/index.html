Jazz MIDI Application

JMA is a learning application centered around Jazz theory. Using your MIDI keyboard, you can go through any exercise as much as you need to learn and reinforce the essentials. Each exercise tracks accuracy and speed to provide feedback, and the list of current exercises include:

To begin, check your MIDI device by clicking the button below. Once you've tested your ability to connect, you are free to start any exercise.

Excercise: Something

Move through the major keys beginning at C, and move down in fourths until you have done every key. You are free to play at any octave. There will be a display to show you which scale you are on, and how many you have left to do. Anytime you have a wrong answer, the notes you are missing will be shown underneath to help. At the end, your time to finish and accuracy will be displayed. To start the excercise, press any key on your MIDI!
