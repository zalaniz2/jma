Jazz MIDI Application

JMA is a learning application centered around Jazz theory. Using your MIDI keyboard, you can go through any exercise as much as you need to learn and reinforce the essentials. Each exercise tracks accuracy and speed to provide feedback, and the list of current exercises include:

- Chords (Major/Minor/Dominant 7th, triads)

- Scales (Major scales, Blues scales)

- Custom chord section

To begin, check your MIDI device by clicking the button below. Once you've tested your ability to connect, you are free to start any exercise.
